Tips on how to use this To-Do List effectively because sometimes we need a lending hand 😂:
Enter your Life Changing Event and then click that Awesome Button!
Your To-Do List will grow and so will your responsibilities.
As your List grows you can click your completed task and it will be crossed out with a checkmark and a blue background.
If you were too ambitious and added too many tasks, just click the x button to the right of your task to obliterate that mistake.
When you completed all your tasks or just want to start fresh, hit that Unclutter Items button to start some more Life Changing Events!
